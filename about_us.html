About Us

Nageeb is an e-learning app that makes education fun and accessible for kids with interactive lessons and activities.

Who’s Nageeb?

Nageeb is an innovative e-learning app designed to make education engaging and accessible for kids. It helps students book sessions with teachers and enables teachers to easily receive and manage these requests. Through interactive lessons and fun activities, Nageeb fosters a love for learning in every child.

Our Core Principles

Providing Tailored Learning Paths That Adapt to Each Student’s Individual Learning Style and Pace

Our Mission

To make education fun and accessible for kids through interactive and engaging e-learning experiences.

Our Value

Empowering every child with engaging and accessible learning experiences.

Our Vision

To inspire a lifelong love of learning in every child through innovative and engaging e-learning solutions.

Our Approach

Personalized, interactive, and secure learning experiences designed by experts.
